Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

VideoDrop is a free video downloader tool. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little data as possible.

What We Collect

  • Video URLs: The links you submit are sent to our server to process your download request. We do not store these URLs after the download is complete.
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp) are collected for security and abuse prevention. These are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Downloaded Files: Temporary video files are automatically deleted from our server within 30 minutes of creation.

What We Don't Collect

  • No account or registration required
  • No personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • No cookies for tracking purposes
  • No browsing history or download history stored

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense: We display ads through Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for DNS and security, which may process connection data. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties beyond the services listed above.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at privacy@videodrop.live.
